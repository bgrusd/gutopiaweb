Gutopia

Built by someone who lives with Crohn’s disease. Gutopia is designed specifically with IBD (Crohn’s & ulcerative colitis) in mind. It’s your private, AI-powered gut-health companion: log meals, medications (including infusions), and symptoms in seconds. Then see clear, personal insights, lag-aware correlations (0–7 days) to capture delayed reactions, cycle tracking for infusion/flare cadence, and a 3–7 day flare-risk window to help you plan ahead. All core analysis runs on your device for privacy; you control exports and backups.
