Why Gutopia Exists

I'm Asher, I'm 17, and I have Crohn's disease.

A few years ago, I started keeping notes on my phone what I ate, how I felt, when my symptoms acted up. It was messy, hard to read, and honestly kind of depressing to scroll through. But it was the only way I could try to figure out what was triggering my flares.

Every other health app I tried felt like it was made for someone tracking their morning jog or daily protein intake. Nothing felt built for people like us people who need to know if that meal from four days ago is why we feel terrible today. People who need to track infusions, not just vitamins. People who want privacy because gut health is personal.

So I decided to build something better.

Gutopia is what I wish I had from the start: quick logging, real pattern recognition with lag awareness, flare forecasting, and everything running privately on your device. No judgment, no data mining, just insights that actually help.

I built this for me. But I'm sharing it because I know I'm not the only one who needs it.

Asher
